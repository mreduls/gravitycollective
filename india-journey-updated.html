15-Day Transformative India Experience

21st October onwards! From Spiritual Awakening to Startup Innovation

Days 1-2

Rishikesh & Haridwar

Spiritual awakening meets adventure sports

Experience authentic spiritual practices with yoga sessions by the sacred Ganges, meditation with Himalayan backdrop, and evening Aarti ceremonies connecting you to India's spiritual essence.

Navigate the rapids of the Ganges with expert guides, experiencing the perfect blend of spiritual serenity and adventure excitement in the foothills of the Himalayas.

Days 3-4

Delhi

Capital's flavors and political heritage

Embark on a culinary adventure through Old Delhi's bustling lanes, sampling authentic chaats, parathas, and traditional sweets while soaking in the vibrant street culture.

Meet influential politicians and government officials in Delhi's corridors of power, experiencing India's political system and democracy in action.

Days 5-6

Agra

Iconic wonder and festive traditions

Witness the eternal symbol of love bathed in the soft morning light, as the Taj Mahal reveals its ethereal beauty in the tranquil early hours before the crowds arrive.

Celebrate Diwali with a local family, participating in traditional rituals, making rangoli patterns, and enjoying authentic festive meals in an intimate home setting.

Days 7-9

Jaipur & Udaipur

Royal heritage and cultural celebrations

Explore the architectural marvels of Rajasthan's royal palaces, where intricate craftsmanship tells stories of bygone eras and regal splendor continues to inspire awe.

Immerse yourself in the colorful spectacle of a traditional Rajasthani wedding, experiencing centuries-old customs, vibrant ceremonies, and the warm hospitality of Indian families.

Days 10-12

Mumbai

Entertainment capital meets business hub

Learn iconic Bollywood moves from industry professionals, experiencing the energy and passion that makes Indian cinema a global phenomenon in the heart of the entertainment capital.

Visit the beating heart of India's economy at the National Stock Exchange, understanding how this financial powerhouse drives the nation's economic growth and global market presence.

Days 13-14

Bangalore

India's Silicon Valley experience

Meet innovative entrepreneurs and founders in India's tech capital, experiencing the dynamic startup ecosystem that's reshaping the global technology landscape from Bangalore's innovation hubs.

Experience Bangalore's vibrant nightlife scene, where tech professionals unwind in stylish clubs and rooftop bars, creating the perfect blend of work hard, party harder culture.
